Our Natural Prestige Lip Glow Treatment Shimmer is a luxury lip care product that provides deep hydration while enhancing your lips with a subtle, elegant shimmer. The advanced formula combines the nourishing benefits of natural oils and botanical extracts with the visual enhancement of mineral-based shimmer.

This product was developed after extensive research into lip care needs, finding the perfect balance between treatment and aesthetic enhancement. The formula addresses multiple concerns including dryness, fine lines, and lack of natural color, all while providing a beautiful finish that can be worn alone or as a base for other lip products.

The subtle shimmer catches light beautifully, creating the appearance of fuller, more youthful lips without the need for invasive procedures or heavy makeup. Our customers report their lips feeling more comfortable throughout the day, even in harsh weather conditions.

Key Ingredients:

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Provides intense hydration and helps lips retain moisture
  • Shea Butter: Nourishes and softens lips
  • Vitamin E: Antioxidant protection against environmental damage
  • Jojoba Oil: Lightweight oil that conditions without feeling greasy
  • Natural Minerals: Create a subtle shimmer effect without synthetic glitters
  • Botanical Extracts: Including chamomile and aloe to soothe and condition

Full Ingredients List: Ricinus Communis (Castor) Seed Oil, Lanolin, Beeswax, Cetyl Alcohol, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Mica, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Sodium Hyaluronate, Tocopheryl Acetate, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Extract, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, Rosmarinus Officinalis (Rosemary) Leaf Extract, Titanium Dioxide, Iron Oxides.

Directions:

  1. Start with clean, dry lips. If needed, gently exfoliate to remove any dry skin.
  2. Apply a small amount of the lip treatment directly to lips.
  3. Spread evenly using the applicator or your fingertip.
  4. Allow a moment for the product to settle and the shimmer to distribute naturally.
  5. Reapply throughout the day as needed for additional hydration and glow.

Tips for Best Results:

  • For an enhanced effect, apply a slightly thicker layer in the center of your lips to create dimension.
  • Can be worn alone for a natural look or under/over your favorite lipstick.
  • For evening looks, add a second layer for more noticeable shimmer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shelf life of this product? +
The Natural Prestige Lip Glow Treatment has a shelf life of 12 months after opening. The packaging includes a PAO (Period After Opening) symbol indicating this.
Is this product suitable for sensitive skin? +
Yes, our formula is designed to be gentle and suitable for most sensitive skin types. However, if you have specific allergies, please review the ingredients list carefully.
How does the shimmer look on mature lips? +
The shimmer in our lip treatment is specifically designed to be flattering on all ages. It provides a subtle luminosity that helps lips appear more youthful and full without settling into fine lines.
Can I use this under lipstick? +
Absolutely! Many customers use our lip treatment as a hydrating base under their favorite lipsticks. Allow it to absorb for about a minute before applying lipstick for best results.
Does this product have any fragrance? +
Our lip treatment has a very subtle natural vanilla scent from botanical extracts, not from artificial fragrances. It's designed to be pleasant but not overpowering.
